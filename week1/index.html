Primary Control

When creating an app, make sure you put the nav bar at the bottom of the screen, so it is reachable. Placement matters.

*If you put a box around the Menu, you will get more clicks.
*Creating moblie first forces simplicity
*Use placeholders and not labels before the box to save on space. After input placed move the placeholder to the top of the box so the client can see what information was needed vs what they inputed in.
*Enable the auto complete to help the forms be filled out faster.
